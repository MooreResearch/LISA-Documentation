This method calculates and returns \( \partial \dot{v} \over \partial \chi_{1 \ell} \).

Technical description:
The purpose of this code is to determine \( \partial \dot{v} \over \partial \chi_{2 \ell} \): the rate of change of \( \partial v \over \partial \chi_{2 \ell} \) with respect to a dimensionless time \( \tau_s = \frac{t_{s}}{M} \) in the source frame. See the theororetical description for information about how a formula for \( \partial \dot{v} \over \partial \chi_{1 \ell} \) was derived.

Theoretical description:
The derivation of a formula for \( \partial \dot{v} \over \partial \chi_{1 \ell} \) can be found in Equation (5) of the DerivativeExpressions document. 
Usage locations:
This method is used in the InitDvdχ1ℓ method of the Evolver class to initialize the first two values of \( \partial v \over \partial \chi_{1 \ell} \), it is also used in the DoStep method to propegate \( \partial v \over \partial \chi_{1 \ell} \) through time.
