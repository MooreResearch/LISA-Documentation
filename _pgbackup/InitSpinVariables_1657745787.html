InitSpinVariables

MethodSpin CaseEvolver

This method initializes the spin variables \( \iota \), \(\alpha\), \( \hat{L}_N\), \( \hat{\chi}_1 \), and \( \hat{\chi}_2 \) by setting their past and present values. 

Technical description:
This method initializes the spin variables \( \iota \), \(\alpha\), \( \hat{L}_N\), \( \hat{\chi}_1 \), \( \hat{\chi}_2 \), \( \vec{\Omega}_1 \), and \( \vec{\Omega}_2 \) by setting their past and present values. The past values of \( \iota \), \( \alpha \), \( \hat{\chi}_1 \) and \( \hat{\chi}_2 \) are set using user inputs. The past value of \( \hat{L}_N \) is calculated using  \( \hat{L}_N = \left[\sin(\iota)\cos(\alpha), \sin(\iota)\sin(\alpha) , \cos(\iota) \right] \). The past values of \( \vec{\Omega}_1 \) and \( \vec{\Omega}_1 \) are obtained by calling the Get立1Coef and Get立2Coef methods using \(v_0\) as the parameter and then multiplying the result by  \( \hat{L}_N\). The present values of \( \hat{L}_N\), \( \hat{\chi}_1 \), \( \hat{\chi}_2 \), are calculated using Euler's method, and the present values of  \( \iota \) and \(\alpha\) are calculated using equation (1.32).
