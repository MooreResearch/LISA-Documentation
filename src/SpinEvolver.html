SpinEvolver

ClassSpin Case

This class initializes and evolves \(\iota\) and \(\alpha\), as well as \(\dot{\alpha}\) and the components of \(\hat{\chi_1}\) and \(\hat{\chi_2}\).

Technical Description:
The purpose of this class is to evolve the angular momentum of the star or black hole system; it has five methods to accomplish this. Firstly, it has the Constructor which intializes \(\iota\) and \(\alpha\), it then has three methods which aid in the calculations of the evolution of \(\hat{L}_N\), and finally the SpinDoStep method which propagates \(\iota\) and \(\alpha\) by propagating \(\hat{L}_N\) and then recalculating \(\iota\) and \(\alpha\).
