DoStep

dτ As Double

 N/A Takes a past and present value of \(v\), \(\partial v \over \partial δ\), \(\partial v \over \partial χ_{1ℓ}\), and \(\partial v \over \partial χ_{2ℓ}\), and, using the centered difference equation, calculates the future values. Then sets the present value to the past value and the future value to the present value.
InitDvdδ N/A N/A This method is called by the Evolver constructor, and it initializes \(\partial v \over \partial δ\) by setting up the first two values.
